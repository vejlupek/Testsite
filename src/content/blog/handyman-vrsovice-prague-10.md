Prague 10 is a large district with varied housing. Vršovice with its classic brick tenements, Strašnice with a mix of panel blocks and older houses, Záběhlice with family homes and newer developments. I work in Prague 10 regularly and have plenty of happy customers there.

## Vršovice – brick buildings and refurbished flats

Vršovice is going through a popular transformation – young families and people refurbishing older flats are moving in. The typical calls I get:

- **Painting** after a refurb – finishing work, plaster repairs, clean walls before moving in
- **Kitchen or bathroom furniture assembly** – correctly and safely fixed to the wall
- **Lock replacement and hardware** – security cylinder locks, additional locks
- **TV, shelf and mirror hanging** – precisely, to the millimetre

### Specifics of brick buildings in Vršovice

Brick walls require the right wall plug and technique. Old wooden floors need care when drilling. I know these details – which means the job is done right the first time.
